About Me

2019-07-02

Education

I am a current Ph.D student in Computer Engineering at University of Southern California under professor Viktor K. Prasanna. I have been working in the FPGA/PARALLEL COMPUTING LAB since October 17.

Research Interests

I am interested in the acceleration of computational intensive algorithm and its applications on processors with various architecture. I am currently working on the efficient training and influencing of Graph Convolutional Network and the Software Defined Hardware project.
